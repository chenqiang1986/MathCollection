Use the strings verbatim — lowercase, no rephrasing, no synonyms, no
pluralization changes. Do **not** invent a new category or subcategory. If
nothing fits cleanly, pick the closest category and use `other` as the
subcategory.

`arithmetic`, `algebra`, `geometry`, `trigonometry`,
`calculus`, `linear algebra`, `number theory`, `combinatorics`,
